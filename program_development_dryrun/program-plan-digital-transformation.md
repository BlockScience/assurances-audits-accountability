# Program Plan - Digital Transformation Program

This program plan provides the execution details for Meridian Consulting Group's digital transformation, including work breakdown, schedule, budget, team structure, and risk management.

## Executive Summary

The Meridian Digital Transformation Program transforms the firm from fragmented legacy systems to an integrated cloud platform over 24 months with a \$2.5M investment. The program delivers modern collaboration tools, knowledge management capabilities, secure client portals, and integrated business applications—enabling competitive positioning for enterprise clients, institutional knowledge preservation, and attraction of modern talent.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Microsoft 365 deployment for all 150 staff
- Searchable knowledge repository with institutional expertise
- Client portal capability for enterprise engagements
- SOC 2 Type II certification
- Integrated CRM and time tracking
- 30% reduction in administrative overhead

### Risk Response Plans

#### R1: User Adoption Failure

**Triggers:**
- <50% Teams adoption after 2 weeks
- Training attendance <80%
- Negative feedback in surveys

**Response:**
1. Escalate to Practice Leaders for peer pressure
2. Deploy Champions for 1:1 support
3. Executive communication on expectations
4. Identify and address specific barriers

#### R2: Partner Resistance to Change

**Triggers:**
- Partners vocally negative in meetings
- Partners continue using legacy tools
- Partners block staff participation

**Response:**
1. Exec Sponsor 1:1 conversations
2. Demonstrate client wins enabled by new tools
3. Peer success stories from other partners
4. If needed, governance decision on compliance expectations
