# Tech Lead Role

## Identity

The Tech Lead role is a technical leadership position accountable for architecture decisions and deployment approvals.

## Capabilities

A Tech Lead can:
- Approve production deployments
- Make binding architecture decisions
- Represent team in cross-team meetings
- Mentor and guide team members

## Constraints

- Must document major decisions
- Accountable for team technical quality
- Cannot unilaterally change standards
