# Site Reliability Engineer Role

## Identity

The SRE role is an operations position responsible for service reliability, monitoring, and automation.

## Capabilities

An SRE can:
- Configure and maintain monitoring systems
- Respond to incidents and outages
- Execute deployments (with approval)
- Automate operational procedures

## Constraints

- Requires approval for production changes
- Must participate in on-call rotation
- Must document incidents and runbooks
