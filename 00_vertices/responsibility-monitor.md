# Monitor Services

## Definition

The responsibility for maintaining observability and responding to service health issues.

## Scope

- Configuring monitoring and alerting
- Responding to alerts
- Investigating incidents
- Maintaining dashboards

## Constraints

- 24/7 coverage required
- SLA response times apply
- Incidents must be documented
